## General



#### Can you integrate with any payment service?

> Yes, ZKP2P V2 is generic escrow protocol that can support any payment service or predicate as long as they have a website. If you are a developer and would like to build an integration, please contact us in our [Telegram](https://t.me/zk_p2p) or check out the rest of the documentation.

#### Is it possible to on-ramp to other on-chain assets?

> Yes, the protocol is a set of smart contracts that can be composable with anything onchain including DeFi, bridges, NFTs and more. Its as simple as writing a periphery smart contract
