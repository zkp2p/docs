# Referrals

## What is the Peer referral program?

As a seller providing liquidity on Peer, you earn a share of Peer's fees every time a seller you invited does business. Share your referral code with other sellers — when their liquidity gets filled, your cut is paid to your wallet **automatically, on-chain**.

There's nothing to claim, and it costs the people you invite nothing: your share is **carved out of the fee Peer already charges** — it's never added on top.

## How it works

- Every seller gets a unique **referral code** — share it as a code or an invite link.
- When another seller joins Peer and **redeems your code**, they become your referral.
- From then on, whenever that seller's liquidity is filled, a slice of the standard fee is routed to you — no claiming, no waiting for a payout run.

Referrals go **two levels deep**:

- **Level 1 (direct)** — sellers who used your code.
- **Level 2 (indirect)** — sellers your Level 1 referrals went on to invite.

You earn on both levels. The program stops at two levels.

## What you earn

Your earnings are taken from the standard service fee on filled volume — so your referrals never pay extra. You receive a fixed share of the volume your referrals generate:

| Level | Who they are | You earn |
|-------|--------------|---------:|
| Level 1 | Sellers who redeemed your code | 0.40% (40 bps) |
| Level 2 | Sellers your Level 1s referred | 0.10% (10 bps) |

**Example:** for every $10,000 of liquidity a direct (Level 1) referral fills, you earn **$40**. For every $10,000 an indirect (Level 2) referral fills, you earn **$10**.

:::info
Referral rates are set by the protocol and read live by the app. The numbers on this page are the current defaults and may be tuned over time. Your combined referral share is always drawn from Peer's existing fee — it never increases what your referrals pay.
:::

## Getting paid

- Payouts are **on-chain and automatic** — each time a referral's order settles, your share is sent to your wallet as part of the on-chain fee split. There's nothing to claim.
- You keep earning from a referral for as long as they keep providing liquidity that gets filled.

## Redeeming a code

- To redeem someone's referral code, sign in with your **Peer account wallet** (the embedded wallet Peer creates for you). Imported or external wallets can't redeem a referral code.
- You can redeem **one** referral code — the seller who invited you becomes your Level 1.

## Where to see your referrals

- **Referrals in the app** — your code and shareable link, how many Level 1 and Level 2 sellers you've referred, and your total earnings to date.
