# For Vault Managers

Vaults let you manage conversion rates on behalf of depositors (liquidity providers) on Peer. You set rates across payment methods and currencies, depositors delegate their deposits to your vault, and you can optionally earn a fee on every fulfilled order.

Vaults are non-custodial. Depositor funds stay in escrow at all times.
